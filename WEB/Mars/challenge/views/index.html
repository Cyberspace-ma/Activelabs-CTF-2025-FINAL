Mars Odyssey is a fictional exploration initiative set in the year 2045, where humanity establishes its first colony on the Red Planet.

    Mars Timeline

  • First unmanned mission to Mars lands successfully - 2025
  • Discovery of underground water reservoirs - 2030
  • First manned mission to Mars departs Earth - 2035
  • Establishment of the first Martian colony, "New Horizon" - 2040
  • First Martian-born human - 2045
  • Construction of the Mars Space Elevator begins - 2050
  • First interplanetary trade route established between Earth and Mars - 2055
  • Mars declared an independent colony - 2060
