Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet. It's the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System.

Milky Way

The Milky Way is our galaxy, home to billions of stars, including our Sun. It's a spiral galaxy spanning 100,000 light-years.

Apollo 11

Apollo 11 was the first spaceflight to land humans on the Moon. It was launched by NASA on July 16, 1969.

Hubble Telescope

The Hubble Space Telescope is a large telescope in space that was launched in 1990. It has provided stunning images of space.

Black Holes

Black holes are regions of space where gravity is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape from it.
